To enable FileProvider, add the following inside <application> in AndroidManifest.xml if not already present:

<provider
    android:name="androidx.core.content.FileProvider"
    android:authorities="com.paras.generatedapp.fileprovider"
    android:exported="false"
    android:grantUriPermissions="true">
    <meta-data
        android:name="android.support.FILE_PROVIDER_PATHS"
        android:resource="@xml/file_paths" />
</provider>
